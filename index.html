🔒 100% On-Device — No Servers, No Tracking

📋 Data Collection

FlyDocs stores all your data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers.

🗂️ Document Storage

All documents and images you upload are stored securely on your device using iOS file protection. We do not have access to your uploaded files.

🔔 Notifications

When you enable notifications, reminders are scheduled locally on your device. No notification data is sent to external servers. You can revoke notification permissions at any time in your iOS Settings under FlyDocs.

🤝 Data Sharing

We do not share any of your personal information with third parties. Your data remains exclusively on your device.

🗑️ Data Retention & Deletion

All data is stored locally on your device for as long as you use the app. You can delete all documents and profile information at any time using the "Reset All Data" option in the Profile tab. Uninstalling FlyDocs from your device permanently removes all associated data.

⚙️ Revoking Consent

You may revoke any permissions granted to FlyDocs at any time through your iOS Settings. To revoke notification permissions, go to Settings → Notifications → FlyDocs. To remove all app data, use the "Reset All Data" feature in the app, or uninstall the app from your device.

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at contactflydocs@gmail.com
